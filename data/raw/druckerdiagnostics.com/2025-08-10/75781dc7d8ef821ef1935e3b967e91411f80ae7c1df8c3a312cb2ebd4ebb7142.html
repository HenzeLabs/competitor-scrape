Adaptive & Therapeutic

Adaptive and regenerative therapies require the right equipment to provide the best results. Drucker Diagnostics offers custom centrifuge solutions designed to support a wide range of applications with confidence.

A model for every application

Regenerative medicine covers a wide range of specialized therapies, from wound care and sports medicine to aesthetic treatments. Drucker collaborates with companies to design custom centrifuge solutions that meet the unique requirements of their applications.

The only centrifuge you’ll ever need

Regenerative therapies require equipment that adapts easily. Drucker’s large-capacity buckets handle oversized tubes, while our swappable inserts allow for smaller samples. This flexibility provides maximum versatility across a wide range of large-volume and small-volume applications.

In-house design and development

At Drucker Diagnostics, we design, manufacture, and support our centrifuges in the United States. Our trusted in-house engineering and production teams provide in-house expertise, shorter lead times, and direct collaboration to support your specialized applications.

Featured Adaptive Centrifuges

BOOST 2 Flex

  • 2-tube capacity for midsize tubes
  • Fits up to 1.1 x 4.7 in (2.8 x 12.1 cm)
  • Max speed 3800 RPM, 2000 xg

BOOST 2+ Flex

  • 2-tube capacity for midsize tubes
  • Fits up to 1.9 x 5.4 in or 140 grams
  • Max speed 4400 RPM, 3000 xg
